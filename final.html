To print your University of Illinois final grades, follow the print instructions for your web browser as you would to print any web page.

Note: Current Program: the word NONE will appear under Current Program if you are in a General Curriculum/Foundations program, which requires you to select a major at a later time, or if you have a major but have not yet selected a required concentration.

Enterprise ID and password authentication is required to view personal information.
